How I got here

  1. Picked it up in middle school.
    • Chose trombone partly because the slide looked the most fun
    • Learned to read bass clef before I could parallel park
  2. Got good enough to teach.
    • Started showing other kids the basics
    • Made the video further down this page at age 11
  3. Kept it as a lifelong thing.
    • Still play for fun and to stay sharp
    • Convinced it's the most underrated instrument in any band

Why the trombone

Most instruments give you keys, valves, or frets — discrete buttons that snap you to the right note. The trombone gives you a slide and a prayer. Pitch is continuous, so your ear has to do the work that a piano's hardware does for you. That's frustrating at first and addictive forever: it's the closest a brass instrument gets to singing.
